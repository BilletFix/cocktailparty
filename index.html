The celebration

We invite you to the annual Mad Hatter's Cocktail Party! The table will be a large one, with three crowded together at one corner: `No room! No room!’ they shall cry when they see you coming. `There’s PLENTY of room!’ you will say indignantly, as you sit down in a large arm-chair at one end of the table.
